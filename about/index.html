Club Description
Our Mission

 Artificial intelligence and machine learning is drastically changing the way people interact with the world. Rather than broad computer science, Gunn ML explores the fundamental algorithms of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This club will primarily focus on teaching various concepts of AI and ML to students while having occasional guest speakers talk about where AI is headed next, and its applications in the modern world. Whether you're new to computer science as a whole, or new to the topic of AI, this club will be a great place for you to learn about one of the fastest growing fields in computer science.

 The long term goal of our club is to introduce students to a field in computer science that is skyrocketing in popularity. Used from personalized ad reccomendations to self-driving cars, AI as a field has grown more than 270% in the last 4 years. As AI continues to grow at a rapid pace, we want to be able to contribute to the Gunn community by providing a fun, safe, and obviously, free way for students to dip their toes in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
